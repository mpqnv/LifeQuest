Welcome to LifeQuest

LifeQuest is a web application that simulates personal growth in key life skills.
Managing goals and responsibilities in life such as protecting physical and mental health, staying productive, and achieving career success can be challenging.
LifeQuest brings a new perspective to accomplishing these goals by gamifying this process to make it more entertaining and engaging for the users.

Features

  • Money Manager
    Track your financial status and goals

  • Healthy Habits Tracker
    Create a healthier lifestyle

  • Productivity Arena
    Stay organized

  • Achievement System
    Track your progress and earn virtual badges and rewards
